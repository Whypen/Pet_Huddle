About huddle

Pet safety, local pet community, trusted pet care, groups, chats, and pet records

huddle is built to help pets get help faster, make local support easier to trust, and keep important pet information organised in one place.

huddle combines pet safety, local pet community, trusted pet care, groups, chats, and pet records in one app, so people do not need separate tools for alerts, advice, bookings, and records.

Most core features are free. Paid plans and add-ons are for people who want more reach, more visibility, or extra tools.

huddle is available through the App Store and Google Play in supported markets, and some features can roll out gradually by market and app version.

What is huddle?

huddle is a pet safety, local pet community, and pet care app that brings urgent alerts, trusted connections, and organised pet records into one place.

  • Use the huddle Live Map and huddle Broadcast Alerts for lost pets, stray animals, hazards, and urgent local situations.
  • Use the huddle Social Forum, huddle Discover, huddle Groups, and huddle Chats to connect with nearby pet people.
  • Use huddle Service and huddle Pet Profiles for trusted local pet care and organised pet records.
What is huddle used for?

People use huddle to protect pets locally, connect with nearby pet people, find trusted pet care, and keep pet records organised.

  • Report lost pets and local hazards with map-based alerts.
  • Connect with pet owners, carers, rescue volunteers, and animal lovers nearby.
  • Keep vaccinations, medications, notes, and care history in one place.

Lost pets, alerts, and emergencies

How do huddle Broadcast Alerts work?

huddle Broadcast Alerts send urgent pet-related alerts to nearby users based on your selected reach, which is faster than relying on one chat, one post, or one group alone.

  • Choose the reach that fits the situation, from 5km to 50km depending on plan or add-on.
  • Keep location and urgency together instead of splitting them across separate tools.
  • Use huddle Broadcast Alerts with the huddle Live Map when speed, coverage, and location matter.
What is the huddle Live Map?

The huddle Live Map gives local pet situations a real location, which makes it clearer and more actionable than a general feed post.

  • Show lost pets, stray animals, hazards, and urgent local situations where they actually happened.
  • Give nearby users a clearer local picture than a general feed or one-off message.
  • Post, edit, and remove your own pins in the huddle Live Map.
What are the pin types on the huddle Live Map?

The huddle Live Map uses four main pin types so users can match the alert to the situation quickly.

  • Lost Pet is for a missing pet that needs urgent local visibility.
  • Stray Animal is for a loose or found animal, and Caution is for a local safety risk such as poison or another hazard.
  • Others is for urgent or relevant animal situations that do not fit the other pin types.

  • Open the huddle Live Map, drop a Lost Pet pin, add a recent photo, and send a Broadcast Alert.
