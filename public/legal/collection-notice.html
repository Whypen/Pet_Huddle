Personal Information Collection Notice

Effective date: 17 March 2026 · Operator: Huddle

This notice is for use at or before points where Huddle collects personal information, such as signup, login, profile forms, phone checks, payment flows, booking forms, support forms, and location permissions.

1. What Huddle may collect

Huddle may collect account details, phone number, date of birth, profile information, pet information, messages, booking and payment details, location data, device data, and trust or safety information.

2. Why Huddle collects it

Huddle collects personal information to create and secure accounts, provide features, run bookings and payments, support users, personalise the service, measure performance, prevent fraud, and comply with law.

3. Is it required?

Some information is required because Huddle cannot create an account, provide a feature, verify a phone number, process a payment, or respond properly without it. Other information is optional. If required information is not provided, Huddle may not be able to provide the relevant feature or service.

4. Who may receive it

Huddle may share the information with service providers, infrastructure providers, payment providers, app stores, support providers, analytics or advertising partners, legal authorities, and other recipients described in the Privacy Policy.

5. International transfers and rights

Information may be processed outside the user's home country, subject to applicable safeguards where required. For fuller details about data handling, rights, and request routes, see the Privacy Policy and Privacy Choices pages.
