## 1. Eligibility and Age Requirements
- Users must be **18+** for full legal standing and marketplace/legal actions.
- Users aged **16+** may access social features where permitted by local law.
- Users under 16 are restricted from Social/Chat interactions.
- Users must provide truthful DOB and identity information.

## 2. Identity Verification and KYC
- huddle may request identity verification for trust and safety.
- Verification may include:
  - selfie capture
  - government ID capture
  - country and document type metadata
- Verification outcomes: `pending`, `approved`, `rejected`.
- False identity submissions may lead to account suspension or termination.

## 3. Biometric and Document Data Handling
- Biometric and document data are collected only for verification and abuse prevention.
- Data is stored in restricted-access storage with admin review controls.
- Access is limited to authorized reviewers and audited operations.
- Users may request deletion in accordance with legal and operational retention requirements.

## 4. Conduct Standards
- Zero tolerance for:
  - harassment
  - hate speech
  - sexual exploitation
  - grooming or predatory behavior
- Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, and legal escalation.

## 5. Age Assurance and Enforcement
- huddle reserves the right to:
  - restrict features based on age
  - request additional verification for suspected false age claims
  - terminate accounts for underage access or fraudulent verification

## 6. Reporting and Moderation
- Users can report content, profiles, and interactions directly in-app.
- Reports are reviewed with priority based on severity.
- Enforcement actions are logged for auditability.

## 7. Safety by Design
- Role-based access controls and RLS are used to protect private data.
- Marketplace and verification actions are reviewed through controlled workflows.
- Abuse prevention measures (rate limits, policy enforcement) are continuously updated.

## 8. Contact and Updates
- Policy updates are versioned and published in project documentation.
- Material changes are communicated in-app or through account notices.
