Privacy Choices

Effective date: 17 March 2026

This page explains where users can manage privacy choices in the app and how to reach Huddle for requests that cannot be completed in the app.

1. In-app choices

Where available, users can manage parts of their information directly in the app, including profile details, pet details, visibility settings, messaging preferences, notifications, location participation, and some account settings.

2. Privacy and legal requests

For privacy, legal, deletion, access, correction, or complaint requests, email enquiries@huddle.pet.

For general support, email support@huddle.pet.

3. What to include

To help Huddle process a request safely, include the email address or phone number linked to the account, the type of request, enough detail for Huddle to understand the request, and any information reasonably needed to verify identity.

4. How Huddle handles requests

Huddle may verify identity, ask for more detail, refuse or limit a request where allowed by law, retain information where needed for security, fraud prevention, disputes, tax, accounting, or legal compliance, and respond within the time required by law.

5. Complaints

If a user has a privacy complaint, they should contact Huddle first using the privacy route above. Users may also have the right to complain to a regulator.
