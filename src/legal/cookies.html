Cookies and Similar Technologies Notice

Effective date: 25 March 2026

Huddle may use cookies, SDKs, pixels, local storage, tags, and similar technologies on its websites and, where relevant, in its app and related services.

1. Why Huddle uses these tools

Huddle may use these technologies to keep the service secure and working, remember settings and preferences, measure usage and performance, and support analytics, recommendations, advertising, and personalisation.

2. Choices

Depending on the technology and where a user lives, choices may be available through browser settings, device settings, app permissions, cookie banners, privacy controls, or opt-out tools. Where consent is required by law before non-essential technologies are used, Huddle will ask for it.

3. More information

For broader information about data use, see the Privacy Policy. For request routes and privacy controls, see Privacy Choices.
