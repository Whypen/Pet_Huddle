Community Guidelines

Effective date: 25 March 2026

Huddle is for pet people, not chaos. These rules apply across profiles, pet profiles, photos, bios, messages, reviews, comments, map features, bookings, listings, support interactions, and every other part of the service.

1. Be real

Do not impersonate anyone. Do not fake your age, identity, qualifications, service experience, or trust status. Do not create duplicate or evasion accounts.

2. Protect people and pets

Do not threaten, harass, bully, stalk, exploit, or intimidate anyone. Do not post or send violent, hateful, sexually exploitative, abusive, or illegal content. Do not endanger people or pets.

3. Respect privacy

Do not share someone else's private information without permission. Do not leak phone numbers, addresses, payment details, identity materials, or private conversations. Do not misuse location features to track or intimidate anyone.

4. No fraud or scams

No phishing, charge abuse, fake reviews, fake bookings, fake claims, or fraudulent listings. No attempts to bypass payment, safety, moderation, or evidence systems. No deceptive off-platform payment schemes.

5. Keep content lawful and accurate

Only share content you have the right to share. Keep important profile, pet, service, and booking information accurate where it affects trust or safety.

6. Respect age gates

Huddle is 13+. Discover and stranger chat are 16+. Do not try to bypass these age limits.

7. Follow the law

Follow all applicable laws and animal-welfare rules. Do not use Huddle for unlawful commerce, harassment, fraud, or unsafe conduct.

8. Enforcement

If users break these rules, Huddle may remove content, limit features, hold payments, revoke trust signals, suspend accounts, or permanently ban access.

9. Reports and appeals

Use in-app reporting tools where available. For support routes outside the app, see the Privacy Choices page.
